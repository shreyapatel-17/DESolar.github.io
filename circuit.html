Solar Sun Tracker


To have an efficient harvest of energy coming from the sun, our group will design a solar tracker that will track the sun from east to west using 2 LDR powered by an Arduino with the implementation of fuzzy logic control. The 2 LDR will be the input to distinguish the intensity of the light of the sun and Servo motor will be responsible for the diversion of the face of the solar panel from east to west.

Distance Measurement Using Ultrasonic Sensor


The ultrasonic sensor emits a high-frequency sound pulse and calculates the distance depending upon the time taken by the echo signal to travel back after reflecting from the desired target.Ultrasonic sensors can measure the distance to a wide range of objects regardless of shape, color or surface texture. They are also able to measure an approaching or receding object.
